How Car Features Affect Fuel Efficiency – Junu Jeon

This narrative visualization explores how car weight, engine type, and model year influence fuel efficiency. The interactive slideshow begins with a guided overview of key trends and ends with an interactive chart to explore how MPG has changed over time.

The dataset used is derived from the Auto MPG dataset from Carnegie Mellon University’s StatLib library.
